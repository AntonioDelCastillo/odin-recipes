Wonderful Mulled Cider for the coldest days

Description

The ice was thick as the sole of my leather boots, inside of them, my toes where closer to being a pack of popsicle rather than part of an human being...

But as soon as I entered home, I knew everything was fine. The scent of the clove kicked right into my face. I bet no one can resist to take a sip of this drink when the arrive home in a windy winter night.

Ingredients

  • 2 quarts of apple cider
  • 20 whole cloves
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 12 sticks of cinnamon
  • 14 whole allspice
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt

Steps

  1. Grind finely half of the cloves and cinnamon sticks
  2. Add a cup of apple cider to the resultant powder
  3. Mix this liquid with the rest of ingredients
  4. Boil it for 20 to 30 minutes and let it rest for 1 hour
  5. Strain the cider and present it with an orange slice
