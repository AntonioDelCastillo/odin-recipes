Second breakfast? I'm in

Description

Bilbo Baggins was a hobbit who lived in the peaceful Shire. He was known for his love of good food and his particular fondness for tea cakes. Bilbo's tea cakes were a special treat that he made for his friends and family. They were a delicious blend of butter, sugar, and spices, and were baked to perfection in Bilbo's own oven. Whenever Bilbo had guests over for tea, he would always serve his famous tea cakes as a special treat. His friends and family couldn't get enough of them, and they became a beloved tradition in the Baggins household.

So the next time you're in the mood for a sweet and savory treat, try making your own batch of Bilbo Baggins' tea cakes!

Ingredients

  • zest of one lemon (or orange if you prefer)
  • 3/4 c. butter
  • 1 c. sugar
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 1/4 c. flour (any soft flour will do)
  • 1 tsp. baking powder
  • 1 tsp. salt
  • 1/2 c. dried cranberries or currents
  • juice of one lemon(orange)

Steps

  1. Cream together butter, sugar and zest. Stir together dry ingredients in a separate bowl. Add the eggs to the butter mixture one at a time, beating well after each.
  2. Add dry ingredients and lemon(or orange...) juice and mix Just Until Combined.
  3. Spoon into greased loaf pan and bake at 350 degrees for 30-45 min.
  4. Wrap and store overnight before serving to Gandalf for Afternoon Tea.
